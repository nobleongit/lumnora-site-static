Mission

Turning vesicle messaging into routine diagnostics

Lumnora delivers a compact imaging flow cytometer that listens to the extracellular vesicle traffic of every cell. In one automated run, labs gain the sharpness of super-resolution microscopy, the sensitivity of light-sheet microscopy, the throughput of cytometry, and AI-ready datasets that translate straight into clinical programs.
